Mission & Vision

Mission: To create world-class professionals in healthcare, focusing on geriatric care, healthcare assistance, and nurse training. We aim to develop compassionate, highly skilled individuals recognized by international bodies.

Our mission is to provide practical, hands-on training in a state-of-the-art facility, ensuring that every trainee has the confidence and expertise to work globally in hospitals, care facilities, and homes. We prepare our students to meet global healthcare demands with integrity, care, and skill.

Vision: To train over a thousand professionals in the next few years who will stand out among their peers and become leaders in the healthcare sector worldwide. Our vision is to transform the healthcare landscape by providing top-tier training that is internationally recognized, creating professionals who make a real difference in people's lives.

About the Program

Our HCA diploma program incorporates theory and hands-on practical training in a highly equipped training facility. In this 8-week program, individuals will learn how to support people who are dependent on health workers due to unforeseen medical conditions. Our course is designed to equip you with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the healthcare industry.

Learning Outcomes

  • Safety and mobility
  • Body systems
  • Assisting with personal hygiene of patients
  • Household management, nutrition, and hydration
  • Assisting with medications
  • Providing person-centered care and comfort
